The Accommodations at Atlantic Trails

Hiking

Atlantic Trails Resprt has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes. The table below lists some of our guided hikes and their departure times from the main office. Sign-up in the office for any or all of the hikes during your stay a Atlantic Trails. Our guides will point out many interesting features and landmarks during your hike.

Tour Start Time Description Guide Time
6:00 am 2 mile hike on the upper ridge - watch the sun rise. Stephanie 2 hours
8:00 am 4 mile hike in the state park. Transportation leaves from main office. Roger 3 hours
1:00 pm 6 mile hike over the full trail and the beach head. Some light climbing Stephanie or Heidi 4.5 hours

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are avilable for guest use. The table provides a summary of our kayaks. Be sure of your kayaking skills. Check the links to identify your skill level. If you have any questions, please contact Dave in the main office.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Atlantic Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week. The table below lists some of these trips. See the main office for more details.

Tour Start Time Description Commonly Seen
Birds		 Guide
5:30 am Tuesdays Only - September thru November 1.5 mile hike on the upper ridge. Lasts about 1 hour Green Herons, Northern Harriers, Ospreys Stephanie
6:00 am Saturday and Sunday Only - March thru June 3 mile hike on the beach. Lasts about 2 hours. Cormorants, Canadian, Geese, Least, Sandpiper Heidi
5:30 am Thursdays Only - June thru August 3 mile hike in the state park. Some light climbing. Lesser Yellowlegs, Willet, Black-bellied Plover Stephanie or Heidi
